Biography

    A few things about me, I graduated highschool from Olympus Highschool in Salt Lake City in 2017. Following my graduation I was able to play rugby that whole summer, which is my favorite sport to play, for the the Utah Lions Rugby club. That August I left for 2 years to serve a LDS mission in Veracruz, Mexico. There I learned of the culture, taught people and was able to learn the Spanish language. After I returned I started school at the University of Utah with a declared Major of Chemical engineering. I was able to complete about 2 years before Covid hit, when it did I took a leave from school because I wasn't readily available to learn. Now I am involved in the Full Stack Development Bootcamp and have been loving every minute of it.
